Meet Our Healthy Cats

All our breeding cats came from long lines of Champion and Supreme Champion Bloodlines, and are full TICA Pedigreed Cats. All our cats are screened annually by our USDA accredited veterinarians for health. All our breeding adults are FIV, FeLv, HCM normal, and PK Def N/N.

King Cali

King Rafi

King Rafi is one of my favorite king. He has a very sweet personality and a calm temperament.

King Simba

King Simba is an amazing, beautiful boy. We call him Simba because he is "cuddly as a soft teddy bear" and is just as playful as a real one. He loves to play tag! He makes such beautiful well tempered baby kittens. Oh and those eyes and perfect paws. He's such a sweetie.

King Max

King Max is a gorgeous cat with an amazing personality.

Queen Bella

Queen Angel

We love our Angel. She has a calm, loving personality. She is extremely easygoing and then ready to play whenever you are. She has very small front gloves, but perfect back laces. She produces some very beautiful Chocolate and Seal Point babies with perfect gloves and laces. She comes from International Grand Champion lines. I just loved her for her sweet gentle face.

Queen Magie

Magie is a perfect example of her breed. She is very curious, and playful and gets on well with other animals. She is gentle as well as affectionate. She is a lovely Blue Point. She makes gorgeous Blue Point and Seal Point Babies.

Queen Tessa
