Frequently Asked Questions

Its hard not to fall in love with these Wonderful Adorable Loving Companions and we get asked a lot of questions which is why we try as much as possible to educate everyone who is interested.

Do Birman cats make good pets?

Yes. The typical Birman cat personality is affectionate, friendly, gentle, and curious. Because they have such an easy-going and kind demeanor, these cats are a wonderful choice for families with children or households with other cats or dogs.
